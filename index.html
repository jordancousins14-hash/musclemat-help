Shipping & Delivery

Orders placed before 1pm are usually dispatched the same day. Delivery typically takes 1–3 business days for metro areas and 3–10 business days for non-metro areas. During busy periods like Christmas, shipping may take a little longer.

Want an estimate to your postcode? Use the AusPost delivery calculator — our dispatch postcode is 2164.
A pre-order means the item is temporarily out of stock, but you can still secure your order and it will be dispatched as soon as new stock arrives. The estimated dispatch date is shown on the product page, at checkout, and in your confirmation email.
A few common reasons your order may not have shipped yet:
  • It's a pre-order — check the product page to confirm.
  • Peak/holiday period — processing times can be extended during sales and holidays.
  • Order issue — incomplete address, payment error, or similar. If it's been more than 3 business days and none of the above apply, email us at info@musclemat.com.au.
Yes, this is normal! Because our products are bulky, multi-item orders are sent in separate parcels. AusPost usually delivers them together, but sometimes they arrive a day or two apart.

To track your other parcel, click the tracking link in your dispatch email and look for the "Item 1 of 2" dropdown — select "Item 2 of 2" to see the second parcel's status.

If the second parcel hasn't arrived within 5 business days of the first, please email us at info@musclemat.com.au.
Our head office is in Brisbane and our warehouse is in Sydney. By warehousing in Sydney — Australia's most central population hub — we can get orders to most customers faster.

Product Questions

Acupressure Mat — Remove the inner foam. Hand wash the outer cover only with mild detergent. Hang to air dry. No iron, bleach, or tumble dry.

CloudPad — The cover is machine washable. Unzip, turn inside out, gentle warm cycle with mild detergent, air dry flat. The inner foam is not washable.

Duvet — Cold gentle machine wash, separately. Line dry in shade. No bleach, tumble dry, or iron.

Bed Sheets — Cold gentle machine wash, max 30°C, mild liquid detergent. Air dry is best. For stains, soak in 1-part vinegar to 4-parts water for one hour then wash.

Mattress Topper — Spot clean with liquid detergent and a damp cloth for minor stains. For deeper clean, cold/warm hand wash or large-capacity laundromat machine on a short gentle cycle. Air dry in the sun. No heat, no dry cleaning.

Relax Mat — Wipe spills with a damp cloth. Use a small amount of gentle detergent for stubborn stains. Vacuum regularly. Don't place heavy furniture on it. Full care guide →

Weighted Blanket (cover) — Remove inner, cold machine wash, gentle cycle, hang to air dry. No heat. For the inner: vacuum, then spot clean if needed with a diluted laundry spray. Ensure fully dry before use.

Mattress Topper

If your mattress is already deep, or your sheets are tight-fitting, you may need deeper sheets after adding the topper. Avoid sheets that are very tight as they'll compress the topper and reduce its comfort.

We sell Australia's deepest bed sheets at 50cm — shop them here.
Our toppers are quite temperature-neutral — loved by customers in Far North Queensland and Tasmania alike. For cooler months, pair with flannelette sheets. For warmer months, go for cotton, linen, or bamboo.
Very popular for caravans! Our topper has square corners (unlike some rounded caravan mattresses), but it's flexible enough that this isn't an issue once you put sheets on.

If your mattress isn't a standard size, measure it and match it as closely as possible to our available sizes. If you're between two sizes, go with the larger one. View dimensions →
Get the same size as your mattress — a Queen bed gets a Queen topper. If your mattress falls between two sizes, measure the width and length and go with the closest match. When in doubt, size up.

A mattress topper is one of the most cost-effective ways to upgrade your sleep. It adds comfort and plushness, evens out minor dips or firmness issues in your existing mattress, and extends its lifespan. More than 50% of Australians now use one.

In very rare cases, the non-slip base can react with older floor polish that's already breaking down, leaving small black marks. We recommend testing the mat on an out-of-the-way spot for a few days before placing it in a main area.
